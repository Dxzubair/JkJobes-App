Privacy Policy — JK Job Alerts

Last updated: 21 June 2026
The short version: JK Job Alerts doesn't collect, store, or share any personal information about you. There's no account, no analytics, no advertising. Everything the app saves is kept only on your own device.

What this app does

JK Job Alerts is a job-notification app for candidates in Jammu & Kashmir. It checks public government and institutional websites (JKSSB, JKPSC, universities, medical colleges, and a few job-listing aggregators) for new recruitment notices, and shows them to you in one place. You can save postings for later and choose to receive a notification when a new one appears.

Information we collect

We don't collect any information about you. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up of any kind — the app doesn't know who you are.
  • No name, email, phone number, or location is requested or stored.
  • No analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs are included in the app.
  • No data is sent to us, to advertisers, or to any third party for any purpose.

Information stored on your device

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your phone, using Android's standard local storage (Room database and DataStore). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else:

What's storedWhy
Job postings fetched from public sourcesSo the app works offline and doesn't need to re-download the same listings repeatedly
Jobs you choose to bookmarkSo your saved list persists between app sessions
Your notification on/off preference and check-frequency settingSo your settings persist between app sessions

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of this data, since it only ever existed on your device.

Permissions the app requests

PermissionWhat it's used for
Internet accessTo fetch public job notifications from the source websites listed above
Network stateTo check whether the device is online before attempting a background refresh
Notifications (Android 13+)To show you an alert when a new job posting is found, only if you've turned this on in Settings

The app does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, files, or any other device data.

Background activity

The app periodically checks the source websites for new postings in the background (you control how often, in Settings — every 3 hours, every 12 hours, or daily). This only involves the app fetching public web pages; it does not transmit any information about you in the process.

Links to external/third-party websites

Tapping a job posting opens that notice on its original source website (e.g. jkssb.nic.in, jkpsc.nic.in, a university's site) in your browser. Those websites are operated independently and are not covered by this policy — please refer to their own privacy policies and terms if you have concerns about visiting them. We have no control over, and no visibility into, what happens once you leave the app.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information from any user at all.

Data security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of a data breach on our end. Locally stored app data is protected by Android's standard per-app storage sandboxing on your device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds a feature that does collect some information — this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
